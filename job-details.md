About The Company

Waymo is a pioneering autonomous driving technology company dedicated to transforming mobility and enhancing road safety. Originating from the Google Self-Driving Car Project launched in 2009, Waymo has established itself as a leader in the development of self-driving systems. Its core mission is to become the world's most trusted driver by deploying advanced autonomous vehicles that improve access to mobility, reduce traffic-related fatalities, and revolutionize transportation. The Waymo Driver, the company's flagship autonomous driving system, has accumulated over ten million rider-only trips, driven more than 100 million miles on public roads across more than 15 U.S. states, and completed tens of billions of miles in simulation. Through continuous innovation and collaboration, Waymo aims to set new standards in safety, reliability, and efficiency in autonomous driving technology.

About The Role

We are seeking a highly skilled Machine Learning Engineer specializing in Model Optimization to join our Perception team. In this hybrid role, you will be instrumental in enhancing the efficiency and performance of our autonomous driving models. Your primary focus will be on optimizing the computational efficiency of models during training and inference, leveraging hardware-software co-development strategies. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to analyze complex model architectures, optimize real-time inference processes, and improve the deployment of models across onboard and offboard platforms. Your expertise will directly contribute to the robustness, responsiveness, and safety of the Waymo Driver, supporting our mission to create the most trusted autonomous driving system in the world.
